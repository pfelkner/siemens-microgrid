## T. Nguyen-Duc, L. Hoang-Tuan, H. Ta-Xuan, L. Do-Van, and H. Takano, A mixed-integer programming approach for unit commitment in micro-grid with incentive-based demand response and battery energy storage system. In Energies, page 7192, MDPI, Basel, 2022.
[LMU Link](https://opac.ub.lmu.de/PrimoRecord/cdi_doaj_primary_oai_doaj_org_article_5e900238f90c4231be1f6aae2278db78)  
[DOI](https://doi.org/10.3390/en15197192)

The paper compares performance of two classic approaches (**Genetic Algorithm** and **MILP**) in a microgrid with renewable energies and a battery storage in the form of a BESS
- It compares their performance when incentives are used to get the users to save energy as well as when a price-based approach is taken where the cost of electricity varies over time (the latter case uses time of use and very high prizes at peak-demand)
- It models the BESS as a VRB system, not as Lithium battery
- In the compared scenarios, MILP performed a lot better than the Genetic Algorithm
