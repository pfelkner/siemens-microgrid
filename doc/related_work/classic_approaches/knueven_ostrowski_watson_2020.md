## Overview

The choice of  MIP formulation has a large impact on computational effort which is why, research focuses on tightening the formulations of of the unit commitment problem together with  relaxations to reduce the time the tree exploration takes in  branch-and-cut

The paper presents different constraints typical for the unit commitment problem and how to formalize them

According to the paper, unit commitment problems with approximately 1000 generators and 48 time periods can be easily solved by classical industry solvers via MILP
