## Y. Bai, Y. Sui, X. Deng, and X. Wang, Quantum-inspired robust optimization for coordinated scheduling of PV-hydrogen microgrids under multi-dimensional uncertainties. In Scientific Reports, article 29589, Springer, London, 2025.
[LMU Link](https://opac.ub.lmu.de/PrimoRecord/cdi_proquest_miscellaneous_3239116775)  
[DOI](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-025-12280-4)

## Overview
The paper covers photovoltaic (PV) plant combined with hydrogen storage in rural microgrids. It proposes a hybrid approach: a quantum-inspired robust optimization (QRO) framework with elements of policy gradient reinforcement learning and deep Q-learning
- the idea is a self-learning microgrid that proactively adjusts its operations to minimize costs and maximize efficiency

---

## Modelling
Paper models PV generation and energy costs – may be inspiration for us:
- PV generation: Gaussian distribution with a mean of 600 kW and standard deviation of 120 kW
- energy prize: mean value of $50/ MWh and volatility of 15%
- To assess resilience, a 48-hour grid blackout and a week-long extreme weather event that reduces PV output by 40% are introduced in model
