## W. Hong,W. Xu, and F. Teng, F., Qubit-efficient quantum annealing for stochastic unit commitment. arXiv preprint arXiv:2502.15917, 2025.
[LMU Link](https://opac.ub.lmu.de/PrimoRecord/cdi_arxiv_primary_2502_15917)  
[DOI](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2502.15917)

## Overview

The paper looks at stochastic optimization which been proposed as a way to manage uncertainties but which also leads to a high computational complexity

It uses  Quantum Annealing in combination with Benders Decomposition (BD) for splitting up a problem into several sub-problems
