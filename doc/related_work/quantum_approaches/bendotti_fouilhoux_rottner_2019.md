## P. Bendotti, P.Fouilhoux, C.Rottner, On the complexity of the Unit Commitment Problem, Annals of operations research, pages 119-130, Springer Nature, Dordrecht, 2019.
[LMU Link](https://opac.ub.lmu.de/PrimoRecord/cdi_gale_infotracmisc_A573937255)  
[DOI](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10479-018-2827-x)

## Overview
The paper gives a proof that the unit commitment problem is strongly NP-hard
- with constraints only covering  minimum up and down times, the unit commitment problem is polynomial for a fixed number of units n
- when constraints also cover unitary cost or the amount of power, the problem is polynomial for T = 1 (T = time periods), and strongly NP-hard for arbitrary T
